## Key Areas

- **Sello Shopping Centre** — Leppävaarankatu 3-9, 02600 Espoo — Main commercial hub with pharmacies, clinics, eye clinic, physiotherapy, bowling alley, and supermarkets all under one roof.
- **Bertel Jungin aukio** — Central square with ELIXIA fitness centre and Terveystalo Leppävaara clinic.
- **Galleria Leppävaara** — Konstaapelinkatu 4 — Home to K-Supermarket.
- **Läkkisepänkuja area** — Health advice centre (Linkki) and Seikkailupuisto Huippu climbing centre.
